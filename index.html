Our Agency

Was established on 1967 in Benghazi.

With over 50 years of experience, ELAREIBI Clearing and Forwarding Agency is one of the leading logistics agencies in Libya, offering trusted and professional door-to-door shipping solutions. Our agency has proudly worked with several international companies, providing complete services in customs clearance, freight forwarding, and logistics management. We are known for our reliability, efficiency, and strong commitment to delivering shipments safely and on time. Backed by decades of expertise and a dedicated team, ELAREIBI continues to build lasting partnerships based on trust, transparency, and excellence in every operation.

Our Services

Customs Clearing

We provide fast and reliable customs clearance for all your shipments. Our team ensures your goods pass through customs smoothly and on time, minimizing delays and avoiding unnecessary costs. With 50 years of experience, we handle all paperwork and regulations so you can focus on your business.

Forwarding

We offer comprehensive freight forwarding solutions tailored to your needs. From planning routes to coordinating with carriers, we manage the entire process to ensure your shipments are handled efficiently and economically.

Unloading

Our expert team provides professional unloading services, taking care of your cargo with precision and safety. We handle all types of goods, ensuring they are delivered intact and ready for storage or distribution.

Door to Door

Experience the ultimate convenience with our door-to-door delivery solutions. We pick up your goods from the origin and deliver them directly to the destination, saving you time, effort, and hassle.
